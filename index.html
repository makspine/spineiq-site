Workflow

From upload to surgical plan

A surgeon submits imaging and clinical context. The platform returns a complete pre-operative plan within minutes.

01

Upload

Submit de-identified MRI, CT, and/or standing scoliosis X-rays via secure portal.

02

Segment

Multi-class AI identifies vertebrae, discs, neural elements, and surgical anatomy in 3D.

03

Analyze

Compute spinopelvic alignment, correction targets, and implant specifications automatically.

04

Harmonize

Register cross-modal data and repose to the patient's standing alignment for surgical planning.

05

Deliver

Surgeon receives a PDF report, 3D visualization, and optional robotic navigation export.

Integration

Fits into your existing workflow

SpineIQ is designed to integrate with the systems surgeons and hospitals already use.

Robotic Navigation

Export surgical plans directly to robotic platforms including Mazor X Stealth, Globus Excelsius GPS, ROSA Spine, and Brainlab navigation systems.

EMR & PACS

Push results to Epic, Cerner, MEDITECH, and other health system EMRs via HL7 FHIR. DICOM-format segmentation overlays route directly to institutional PACS.

Cross-Modal Fusion

When MRI and standing X-ray are both submitted, the platform performs 2D-3D pose harmonization so the surgeon sees the 3D anatomy in the surgical standing position.
